How it works

A life-changing move,
the right steps…

Your new life in Portugal doesn't begin with a property search. It begins with a conversation. We take the time to understand exactly how you want to live, and then we take you there. The streets, the morning markets, the café where everyone knows your name. Once you feel it, the right home finds you.

01
Remote exploration

We start with a deep conversation about your lifestyle, values, and what the word "home" truly means to you. A personalised area profile is built before you ever book a flight, so your time here counts from the very first day.

02
Discovery trip

Two to four days on the ground together: beaches, restaurants, markets, local schools, and the kinds of neighbours who become lifelong friends. You fall in love with the place first. Everything else follows from there.

03
Property & paperwork

We visit homes together, verify every document, coordinate legal and financial partners, and stand beside you at the notary on signing day. You never navigate the paperwork alone.

04
Integration

Neighbours, doctors, schools, and yes, the local fishmonger who saves you the finest catch of the day. We stay close until Portugal doesn't feel new anymore. Until it simply feels like home.

Good to know

Frequently
asked questions

Which areas of Portugal does Move Easy cover?

We specialise in Cascais on the Atlantic coast near Lisbon, and Sesimbra inside the Arrábida Natural Park, along with the wider Setúbal and Arrábida region.

How much does a relocation package cost?

We work on fixed fees with no commission on property sales: Discovery from €990, Settle from €2,900 and Roots from €5,500. Payment is split 50% on start and 50% on completion.

What is NHR 2.0 and can you help with it?

NHR 2.0 is Portugal's updated tax incentive regime for new residents. Our Roots package includes support with your NIF, bank account and NHR / NHR 2.0 registration alongside trusted accountants.

Is the first call really free?

Yes. The first 30-minute discovery call is free with no commitment, and we typically reply within 24 hours.

How long does relocating to Portugal take?

Most families plan over roughly 12 months from first conversation to settled life. We guide each stage, from remote exploration to full integration.
